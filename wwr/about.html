History

Established in the late 1970s, our white water rafting company emerged as a pioneer in the adventure tourism industry. Founded by a group of outdoor enthusiasts, we began with a single raft and a vision to share the exhilaration of navigating untamed rivers. Over the decades, we grew into a premier destination for thrilling river expeditions, for our commitment

renowned to safety and expert guiding. From navigating gentle rapids for beginners to tackling adrenaline-pumping whitewater for seasoned adventurers, our company has remained dedicated to providing unforgettable experiences while preserving the natural beauty of the rivers we cherish.
